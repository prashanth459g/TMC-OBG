# Modified Ferriman Gallway scoring for hirsutism
- ![[Pasted image 20220622170812.png]]
	- ![[Pasted image 20220622172815.png]]
	- The forearm & Lower leg are excluded as they no longer considered as androgen dependent
	- *0* - vellus hair or absence of terminal hair
		- vellus - soft, short & non pigmented hair
	- *4* - Extensive terminal hair
		- terminal - Coarse, long & pigmented
- **Scoring**
	- <8 - No hirsutism
	- 8 to16 - mild
	- 16 to 25 - moderate
	- \>25 - severe
- *Cook*'s modification of  [[Modified Ferriman Gallway scoring for hirsutism]]
	- in 2011
	- is combination of only 3 areas --> upper abdomen, lower abdomen & chin
	- score >7 is the distinguisher between non-hirsutism & hirsutism
