MITU - Maternal Intensive Treatment Unit

JR Works

  1. All patients need to be intensely monitored and monitoring hourly or 2hrly (depending upon the case) to be filled in mitu monitoring chart. Special ones Grbs monitoring, temp monitoring, hourly output to be added up in chart itself.
  2. Progress notes bd maintained inside casesheet along with case seen by to be written.
  3. All additional information like drain removal, methylpred, orofer administration, blood transfusions, consultations, change of drugs have to be updates along with progress notes.
  4. Transfer out patient with Mitu transfer out notes for the ease of reference from wards (will be useful for you guys only)
  5. If patient on ventilator update ventilator settings in both chart itself and inside also.
  6. Registers:
    • Doctors report book:
      All admission and transfer in to be entered, and when transferred out, progress updated. (Night hs to write down recieval notes, if eventful admission while transferring out patient, update report book; take a photo of transfer out notes if no time and update when you are free)
    • Near miss register:
      For all AN, postnatal cases, confirm if it's near miss. If fits to criteria, must enter in this register. This is very important. Also enter the details in near miss group. You all are added to group.
